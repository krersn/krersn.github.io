About the App

Traditional game, fluid mobile session.

Yanık Card Game Offline brings the Yanık and Yunan variants of traditional Turkish Okey to your mobile device. Play single-player against AI opponents — casual or competitive, your choice.

The app runs fully offline — your data stays on your device and is never sent to servers. Ads are opt-in and only shown with your consent.

Account Integration

Google Account Connection

The app integrates optionally with Google Play Games Services. This feature provides:

  • ◆ Player identity: Your Google account nickname is used to appear on the leaderboard
  • ◆ Achievements: Earned achievements are synced with your Google account
  • ◆ Leaderboard: Your high scores are submitted to the Google Play Games leaderboard
What data we request: Only the games_lite scope — meaning the standard achievement and leaderboard functions of Google Play Games Services. Your email, contacts, or other personal data are not requested and not collected.

Signing in with a Google account is entirely optional. The full game can be used without signing in; only achievement and leaderboard sync are disabled in that case.

⚠️ Age Restriction: This app is intended for users 18 years of age or older. The app contains simulated gambling mechanics (virtual chips, betting). No real money is used; virtual chips have no monetary value and cannot be exchanged.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an internet connection?
No. The app runs fully offline. A connection is only used for optional ad viewing and Google Play Games Services sync.
Does the app use real money?
No. The virtual chips have no monetary value, cannot be purchased, and cannot be exchanged. The app is completely free.
Is my data safe?
Your game data (chip amount, progress, settings) is stored only on your device. It is not sent to servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
What devices does it run on?
Android 8.0 (API 24) and above. The vast majority of modern Android devices are supported.
