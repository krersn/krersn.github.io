About the App

Traditional game, live tables with real players.

Yanik Online Card Game lets you play the Yanik and Yunan variants of traditional Turkish Okey online with real players. You can add friends, create private tables, chat by text or voice, gift chips, and exchange chips for diamonds to buy virtual gifts from the diamond store and send them to your friends.

Online features run on a secure cloud infrastructure (Google Firebase). Your account, chip balance, and progress are backed up in the cloud, so you can continue where you left off even if you change devices.

Account & Online Infrastructure

Account, Sign-in, and Cloud

To use online features, the App creates a secure anonymous account (Firebase Authentication). Optionally, you can sign in with Google to make your account permanent.

  • ◆ Profile: your display name, avatar, and shareable friend code
  • ◆ Cloud sync: chip balance, statistics, and friends list are stored in the cloud
  • ◆ Payment security: purchases are processed by Google Play; your card details are not shared with us
⚠️ Age Restriction: This app is intended for users 18 and over only. It contains simulated gambling mechanics (virtual chips, betting) and real-money purchases. Virtual chips cannot be converted to real money, are non-refundable, and have no value outside the app.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is an internet connection required?
Yes. This is an online game; an internet connection is required to play with real players, chat, add friends, and send gifts.
Can I buy chips? Is it real money?
Yes, you can buy virtual chip packages with real money through Google Play. However, virtual chips have no monetary value outside the app; they cannot be converted to real money or refunded for cash.
Can I gift chips to a friend?
Yes. You can send chips from your available balance as a gift to your friends. Sent gifts cannot be reversed (except where fraud is detected).
What are diamonds and how do I get them?
Diamonds are a second virtual currency you obtain by exchanging your chips at a set rate. You can use diamonds to buy virtual gifts from the diamond store and send them to your friends. Diamonds also have no real monetary value and cannot be cashed out.
How does voice chat work — is my microphone recorded?
Voice chat is optional and requires microphone permission. Your voice is transmitted in real time only to the other participant(s) in the call; it is not permanently recorded. You can turn the feature off at any time from the app's settings.
Where is my data stored?
Your online data (account, chips, diamonds, friends, chat) is stored on Google Firebase cloud infrastructure. See the Privacy Policy for details.
Which devices does it run on?
It runs on Android 8.0 (API 24) and above. The vast majority of modern Android devices are supported.
